## The beginning

I started coding during my studies at CCI Longwy, learning HTML, CSS, and PHP. What started as curiosity quickly became a passion.

## The stack evolution

Over the years, my stack evolved significantly:

- **2020-2022**: PHP, Symfony, jQuery — the classic web stack
- **2022-2023**: Laravel, React, Vue.js — modern frameworks
- **2023-present**: Nuxt, TypeScript, AWS, Rust — full-stack + cloud

::callout{icon="i-heroicons-light-bulb" color="primary"}
The best technology is the one that solves the problem. Not the trendiest one.
::

## What I do today

Currently working at **Jewely x Flippad** as a Web Development Engineer. My day-to-day involves building Laravel APIs, deploying on AWS, and crafting frontends with Nuxt.

## What's next

I'm diving deeper into **Rust** and **cloud architecture**. The goal is to combine systems-level performance with modern web development patterns.
